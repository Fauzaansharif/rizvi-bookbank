Welcome to Rizvi Book Bank

A platform for students to get engineering books easily within the college.

The Rizvi College Book Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline the process of distributing academic books among engineering students. First-year students sign up, make a one-time payment, and receive books that are used throughout their four-year course. Upon graduation, they pass these books down to their juniors, ensuring a sustainable, cost-effective system that reduces waste and makes books readily available within the college. The system categorizes books based on different branches—Electronics & Computer Science, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering—allowing easy access to relevant materials. The platform includes features like user registration, book search functionality, and an intuitive UI to enhance user experience. By digitizing the book-sharing process, this system not only saves students' time and money but also promotes resource optimization within Rizvi College of Engineering.
